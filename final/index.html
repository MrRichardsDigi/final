About Us

We are two students who have struggled with our favorite clothing not having pockets so that is why we made Pocksy. Pocksy is a handsown pocket with a hook on the side. We also sell mini keychanges that can be attached to our pockets. It is held onto your clothing but two magnets on each side of your clothing. It is sized to hold your phone or any other valuables. A cool feature is that in the pocket there is an emergency charger if your phone is about to die. If you are interested in an attachable pocket, shop with Pocksy today!
